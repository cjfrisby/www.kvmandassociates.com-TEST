Home  /  Services

Built for Houston small businesses ready for a real CPA partner.

Owner-operated, growing, somewhere between "I just hired my first bookkeeper" and "I should probably have a CFO" — every service below is scoped for that stage. Flat fees, no long contracts, no surprises.

01 · Tax Planning & Preparation

We work the calendar year, not just tax season. The result is fewer surprises in April — and usually a lot less owed.

  • Federal & multi-state business returns (S-Corp, C-Corp, LLC, Partnership)
  • Personal returns for owners, partners & high earners
  • Quarterly estimates & year-end planning sessions
  • IRS & Texas Comptroller representation
  • R&D credits, QBI deduction, depreciation strategies
Best for: Owners who want a CPA they can call mid-year — not just a preparer they email in March.

02 · Bookkeeping & Accounting

Monthly books, reconciled and clean, with a real human reviewing them — not just software flagging exceptions.

  • Monthly & quarterly bookkeeping (cash or accrual)
  • QuickBooks Online & Xero setup, cleanup, and migration
  • Bank, credit card & merchant reconciliation
  • Custom monthly financials with plain-language summaries
  • Sales tax filings & 1099 prep
Best for: Businesses doing $200K–$10M who want clean books without hiring a full-time bookkeeper.

03 · Outsourced CFO & Advisory

Strategic financial leadership without the strategic-financial-leader salary. We sit in your corner — and ask the questions you haven't thought of yet.

  • Cash-flow forecasting & scenario modeling
  • Annual budgets & rolling 13-week cash plans
  • KPI dashboards & monthly performance reviews
  • Pricing, margin & unit-economics analysis
  • Lender packages, due diligence & exit prep
Best for: Owners doing $1M+ who feel like they're flying half-blind on the financial side.

04 · Payroll Services

Bi-weekly or monthly payroll handled end-to-end, with all the filings the government wants and none of the panic.

  • Full-service payroll setup & ongoing runs
  • 941, 940, W-2, W-3, and 1099 preparation & filing
  • New-hire onboarding & state registrations
  • Workers' comp & benefits coordination
  • Direct deposit, garnishments, multi-state payroll
Best for: Businesses with 1–50 W-2 employees who want to outgrow their DIY setup.

05 · Entity Formation & Structure

The right entity, set up the right way, can save you tens of thousands over time. We'll explain why before we ever set anything up.

  • LLC, S-Corp, C-Corp, and Partnership formation
  • Tax-efficient entity structure analysis
  • EIN registration, state filings, operating agreements
  • S-Corp election timing & reasonable comp planning
  • Restructuring existing entities for growth or sale
Best for: New founders & growing operators ready to formalize.

06 · Specialty & Industry-Specific

Industries we know cold — because we work with a lot of them. Less ramp-up time, more useful answers from day one.

  • Restaurants & hospitality (tip credits, sales tax, COGS)
  • Real estate investors (cost seg, 1031, REPS)
  • Professional services (PSCs, partner draws)
  • E-commerce & multi-state retail
  • Nonprofits & 990 prep
Best for: Operators in niche industries who are tired of explaining their business to their accountant.

FAQ

The questions we get asked the most.

How much does it cost to work with KVM?

It depends on what you need — tax-only relationships are scoped differently than full bookkeeping + advisory engagements, and CFO-level work is scoped individually. Whatever the scope, we'll quote you a flat fee in plain English up front. No hourly surprises.

Do you work with clients outside Houston?

Yes — we serve clients across Texas and in other states. Most of our work happens by video call and shared drives. Houston clients sometimes drop by our office, which we love.

I'm behind on bookkeeping (or taxes). Will you judge me?

Not even a little. A lot of new clients come to us with months — sometimes years — of catch-up work. We'll triage it, get it cleaned up, and file what needs filing. No lectures.

Can you take over from my current accountant?

Absolutely. We do this constantly and have a clean transition process — we'll request prior returns, transfer files, and minimize the awkwardness. (You don't even have to be the one to break up with them.)

Do you do personal taxes only, or do I need to be a business?

We do personal returns, but our sweet spot is business owners and the personal returns that come along with that. If your situation is purely W-2, a tax software is probably the better fit — and we'll tell you that.

How quickly do you respond to questions?

Inside 24 business hours, always. Most things in a few hours. If it's urgent, you can call — and a real person will pick up.

Not sure which service you need?

Tell us about your business — we'll help you figure it out.
