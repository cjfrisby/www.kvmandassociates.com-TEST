People first. Numbers second. Spreadsheets, somewhere around third.

We're a small Houston CPA firm with big convictions about what good accounting actually looks like — and we'd love to tell you more.

Our story

Started in 2008. Still answering our own phones.

KVM & Associates was founded on a simple frustration: most accounting firms treat their clients like file numbers. Our founder had spent years inside one of those firms, watching small business owners get the same generic advice — usually right around April 14th — and figured Houston deserved something better.

Fifteen years later, we're a small, owner-led firm that knows our clients by their first names, their kids' names, and the names of the businesses they've poured their lives into. We work with small-to-mid-sized businesses, professional services firms, restaurants, retailers, and the kind of ambitious entrepreneurs who keep Houston interesting.

We don't pretend to be the biggest firm. We just try to be the most useful one you'll ever work with.

What we believe

Four things we won't compromise on.

Plain English, always

If you don't understand the answer, it isn't an answer. We'll explain it as many times as it takes — without making you feel small for asking.

Proactive, not reactive

Real tax savings happen in October, not April. We talk to clients year-round so the wins are baked in by the time we file.

Relationships over transactions

We'd rather have 200 clients we know well than 2,000 we don't. We say no to growth that would compromise that.

Boring accuracy, every time

Warm doesn't mean sloppy. We're CPAs — the books are clean, the filings are correct, and the deadlines aren't missed.
