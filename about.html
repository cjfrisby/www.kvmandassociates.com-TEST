We're a small Houston CPA firm with big convictions about what good accounting actually looks like — and we'd love to tell you more.

Our story

Started in 2008. Still answering our own phones.

KVM & Associates is run by two partners who have spent their working lives inside this Houston practice: Khan, our CPA, and Thu, our senior accountant. Between them, they bring nearly half a century of experience walking Houston business owners through every situation a small accounting firm sees — the easy ones, the messy ones, and the ones nobody warned the owner about.

That long tenure is why our standards feel a little old-school: clients we know personally, advice we give year-round, work we sign our names to. We're a small, owner-led firm — and we know our clients by their first names, their kids' names, and the names of the businesses they've poured their lives into.

We work with small-to-mid-sized businesses, professional services firms, restaurants, retailers, and the kind of ambitious entrepreneurs who keep Houston interesting. We don't pretend to be the biggest firm. We just try to be the most useful one you'll ever work with.

What we believe

Four things we won't compromise on.

Plain English, always

If you don't understand the answer, it isn't an answer. We'll explain it as many times as it takes — without making you feel small for asking.

Proactive, not reactive

Real tax savings happen in October, not April. We talk to clients year-round so the wins are baked in by the time we file.

Relationships over transactions

We'd rather have 200 clients we know well than 2,000 we don't. We say no to growth that would compromise that.

Boring accuracy, every time

Warm doesn't mean sloppy. We're CPAs — the books are clean, the filings are correct, and the deadlines aren't missed.
